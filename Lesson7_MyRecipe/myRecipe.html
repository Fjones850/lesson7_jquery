Caribbean Delight

Flan

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 can goya sweentened condensed milk
  • 1 can goya evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp goya vanilla extract
  • 1 c sugar

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325.

In small sauce pan bring sugar and half cup of water to boil, until sugar desolves.

Using a pastry brush wipe off any excess sugar that sticks to the pot.

In a medium bowl, whisk eggs until fully blended, then add both milks with vanilla extract until fully blended.

Cook, without stirring, swirling pan, until syrup turns deep golden brown, about 12 minutes.

Divide milke evenly among ramekins.

Carefully add enough hot enough water to baking dish, to come halfway up sides of ramekins; cover baking dish tightly with foil.

Bake until the flan is set but still wiggly in center, bake for about 22-25 minutes. Using tongs, carefully remove ramekins from water bath. Move to the refrigerator to cool completely.

To serve, gently run butter knife around edges of flan; invert onto serving plates. Garnish with raspberries and mint leaves, if desired..
